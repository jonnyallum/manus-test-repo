# Little Jonnys Catering - SEO Implementation Guide

## Overview
This guide outlines the SEO strategy implemented for Little Jonnys Catering to improve visibility on Google for local searches in Emsworth, Hampshire, and surrounding areas.

## What's Been Implemented

### 1. **Schema Markup (JSON-LD)**
- **Local Business Schema**: Identifies your business location, contact info, and service areas
- **Service Schema**: Describes your catering services and offerings
- Located in `client/index.html`

### 2. **Meta Tags & Open Graph**
- **Title Tag**: "Wedding, Wake & Event Catering | Little Jonnys | Emsworth, Hampshire"
- **Meta Description**: Optimized for local keywords and search intent
- **Open Graph Tags**: For social media sharing (Facebook, LinkedIn)
- **Twitter Card Tags**: For Twitter sharing
- **Canonical Tags**: Prevents duplicate content issues

### 3. **Technical SEO Files**
- **sitemap.xml**: Lists all pages for search engines to crawl
- **robots.txt**: Guides search engine crawlers
- Both files located in `client/public/`

### 4. **Local SEO Optimization**
- Location-specific keywords: Emsworth, Hampshire, West Sussex
- Service-specific keywords: Hog roast, pizza van, mobile bar, buffets
- Area served schema markup for local visibility

## Next Steps to Maximize Google Visibility

### Immediate Actions (High Priority)

1. **Claim Your Google Business Profile**
   - Go to https://www.google.com/business/
   - Claim your business listing
   - Add complete business information, hours, photos
   - Verify your location
   - This is CRITICAL for local Google search visibility

2. **Submit Sitemap to Google Search Console**
   - Go to https://search.google.com/search-console/
   - Add your property (littlejonnys.co.uk)
   - Submit sitemap.xml
   - Monitor search performance and indexing

3. **Set Up Google Analytics**
   - Already integrated via Umami analytics
   - Also add Google Analytics 4 for additional insights
   - Track user behavior and conversion paths

4. **Create Location-Specific Landing Pages**
   - Create pages for: Emsworth, Portsmouth, Chichester, Arundel, Worthing
   - Include local keywords and testimonials from those areas
   - Link back to main services pages

### Medium Priority Actions

5. **Build Local Citations**
   - List your business on:
     - Yelp
     - TripAdvisor
     - The Knot (for weddings)
     - Eventbrite
     - Local Hampshire/West Sussex business directories
   - Ensure NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency across all listings

6. **Optimize Images for SEO**
   - Add descriptive alt text to all images
   - Use descriptive filenames (e.g., "hog-roast-wedding-emsworth.jpg")
   - Compress images for faster loading
   - Consider adding image schema markup

7. **Create FAQ Content**
   - Add FAQ section to website with schema markup
   - Address common questions:
     - "How much does catering cost?"
     - "How far do you travel?"
     - "What's included in your service?"
     - "How far in advance should I book?"

8. **Build Backlinks**
   - Get mentioned on local event planning websites
   - Partner with local wedding planners, venues, florists
   - Submit to local business awards/directories
   - Guest post on local blogs

### Long-Term Strategy

9. **Content Marketing**
   - Create blog posts on catering topics
   - Share event planning tips
   - Showcase past events with photos and testimonials
   - Target keywords like "wedding catering tips Hampshire"

10. **Social Proof & Reviews**
    - Encourage customers to leave Google reviews
    - Showcase testimonials on website
    - Build social media presence (already linked to Facebook/Instagram)
    - Share event photos and customer stories

11. **Mobile Optimization**
    - Ensure website is fully responsive (already done)
    - Test on mobile devices regularly
    - Optimize for mobile search intent

12. **Page Speed Optimization**
    - Monitor Core Web Vitals in Google Search Console
    - Optimize image sizes
    - Minimize CSS/JavaScript
    - Use CDN for faster delivery
